OUR LAB
  • Location:Antwerp - Budapest
  • Year:2020-2023
  • Technology:GIA ID100, Yehuda 4.0, Presidium gem tester, microscope, UV tester
  • Diamond grading is a complex procedure that requires appropriate knowledge and practice. The purpose of detailed regulation is to minimize the subjective choices of the individual, creating a report that is consistent and reliable.

    The CIC is following the 4C grading system in many aspects and doing it according to strict rules. The certificate issued by our laboratory provides all stakeholders with sufficient and appropriate information to support a transaction between them.

    Overall, certifying a diamond provides peace of mind to buyers and sellers alike, ensuring that they are making a fair and informed transaction based on the diamond's quality and value. We hope, that it is useful for professional gemologists and appraisers who need a comprehensive reference for grading diamonds or for jewelers, who want to expand their knowledge of diamonds and learn about the factors that impact their value.

    Our mission is to serve the European diamond and jewelry industry at the highest possible standards. The most important element of this is the use of the latest technology and following professional updates.
